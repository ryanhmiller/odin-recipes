German Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 5 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup flour
  • pinch salt

Steps

  1. Turn oven on to 425 degrees
  2. Put butter in 9x16 pan and put in oven while oven heats up
  3. Mix all other ingredients in bowl until well mixed
  4. Once butter in pan is melted, pour mixture on top of butter in pan
  5. Cook for 23-27 minutes, or until golden brown
